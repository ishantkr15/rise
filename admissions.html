Admissions

Join our growing community of learners

Admission Process

1

Inquiry

Contact us via phone, email, or visit the school to inquire about admission.

2

School Visit

Schedule a visit to our campus to see our facilities and meet our staff.

3

Application

Fill out the admission form and submit required documents.

4

Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation and complete fee payment.

Admission Requirements

Documents Needed

  • Birth Certificate (for Nursery admissions)
  • Transfer Certificate (for Class 1 onwards)
  • Aadhar Card of student
  • Aadhar Card of parents
  • Passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • Previous year's report card (if applicable)

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 3+ years as on 1st April
  • LKG: 4+ years as on 1st April
  • UKG: 5+ years as on 1st April
  • Class 1: 6+ years as on 1st April
