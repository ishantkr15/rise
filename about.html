About Our School

Learn about our history, mission, and facilities

Our Story

RISING CHILDREN ACADEMY, RAM LEELA GOUND was established in 2012 and is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. Located in urban Moradabad, our school serves students from Grades 1 to 8 with Hindi as the medium of instruction.

Our Infrastructure

The school has a private building with 10 well-maintained classrooms, 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities, and a separate room for the Headmaster. We have pucca boundary walls and electric connection throughout the campus.

Facilities

  • Functional hand pumps for drinking water
  • 2 boys toilets and 2 girls toilets
  • Library with 125 books
  • Safe and secure environment

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, and Compassion guide everything we do at Rising Children Academy.

Our Faculty

Our dedicated team of qualified teachers is committed to providing personalized attention to each student, ensuring their academic success and overall development.
